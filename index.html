The Story

Brick by brick – it all started with an architect by the name of Otto Wolf.

From breweries to bottling houses, storage facilities and more, Wolf’s innovative designs transformed a neighborhood into an icon. Today, while much of his work has fallen, Otto Wolf’s legacy continues to inspire those living in and building up Brewerytown.

A building by Equinox

OTTO is a flagship project of Equinox, a fully integrated real estate development, property management, general contracting, and property maintenance company. Equinox is committed to creating positive change in our built environment and contributing to the communities where we live and work.

The Neighborhood

Brewerytown,
Philadelphia

The right place, the right time, the right people—nothing happens by coincidence. It was the collective vision of brewers, immigrants, and architects that built this neighborhood.

Today, that history is inspiring a new wave of growth. But it’s not about revitalizing what already exists. This is about writing the next chapter of an already remarkable story. This is about Brewerytown.

31st Street

Once lined with the brick facades of prominent breweries, 31st Street is now a hotbed for development. Fairmount Park, Center City, highways, parkways—the corridor is in the midst of becoming Philadelphia’s most sought after residential area, and OTTO is your chance to be part of it.
